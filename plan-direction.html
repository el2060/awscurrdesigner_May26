Narrative

Synopsis and Aims

This module strengthens escalation judgement in acute-care contexts through integrated simulation, interdisciplinary communication, and evidence-informed decision pathways.

Competency

Learning Outcomes

  • Apply escalation frameworks to complex, time-sensitive patient deterioration scenarios.
  • Justify differential clinical decisions using diagnostic indicators and risk thresholds.
  • Communicate escalation recommendations to interdisciplinary teams with defensible reasoning.
  • Reflect on decision quality and identify mitigation strategies for near-miss escalation events.

Pedagogy

Teaching Strategy

  • Escalation drills with staged uncertainty and role handoffs.
  • Structured simulation debriefs focused on clinical reasoning traces.
  • Peer critique checkpoints for escalation communication quality.
  • Short evidence synthesis bursts before each applied practice cycle.

Governance

AI and Academic Integrity

  • AI-assisted drafting is allowed for outline generation, not final clinical claims.
  • Students must annotate where AI support influenced their reasoning artifacts.
  • Simulation assessments require in-person justification of escalation rationale.
  • Integrity checks focus on reasoning traceability rather than text similarity only.
